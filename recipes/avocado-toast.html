Avocado Toast
Description

Quick, healthy, and endlessly customizable, avocado toast is a nutritious breakfast or snack thatâ€™s ready in minutes.

Ingredients
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread (whole grain, sourdough, or your choice)
  • 1/2 lemon (for juice)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Red chili flakes or seeds (optional for topping)
Steps
  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash with a fork until smooth.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the avocado mash and stir to combine.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on the toast.
  5. Drizzle olive oil or sprinkle chili flakes for extra flavor, if desired. Serve immediately.
