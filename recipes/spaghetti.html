Spagghetti Aglio e Olio
Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with just a few simple ingredients. Perfect for a quick, flavorful meal that comes together in less than 20 minutes!

Ingredients
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
Steps
  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions. Reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water, then drain the rest.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the garlic slices and saut√© until golden (about 2-3 minutes).
  3. Add red pepper flakes and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Add the drained pasta to the pan along with a splash of the reserved pasta water. Toss everything together to coat the spaghetti.
  5. Season with salt, garnish with parsley, and sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top, if desired. Serve hot!
